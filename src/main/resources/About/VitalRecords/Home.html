Supporting

The NIST/CDC CDA Test Suite supports a broad range of testing in support of the HL7 Vital Records Community, including transport, structure and message content validation in accordance with the HL7 CDA® Release 2 Implementation Guide: Vital Records Death Reporting, Release 1 STU 2, US Realm January 2017.

Supported Browsers

The following browsers are supported:

  • Chrome (Recommended)
  • Firefox (Recommended)
  • Safari
  • IE 9 and 10
